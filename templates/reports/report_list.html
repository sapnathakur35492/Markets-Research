Markets NXT — A Montclaire & Stein Venture

A Century of Global Intelligence Behind Every Report

Markets NXT builds on a legacy dating to the early 1900s, when economists Edgar Montclaire and Johann Stein began advising enterprises across Europe and North America on industrial expansion and globalization. Over more than a century, Montclaire & Stein accumulated institutional knowledge spanning economic intelligence and market dynamics across geographies — forming the analytical foundation behind every report we publish today.

Why Country-Level Intelligence Matters

Global markets are not uniform. Regulatory environments, consumer behavior, industrial composition, and competitive dynamics differ significantly by country. Country-specific research helps businesses:

  • Accurately size and forecast a market within a defined geography
  • Understand local demand drivers, supply chains, and competition
  • Navigate country-specific regulatory and policy frameworks
  • Identify entry points, opportunities, and risk factors at a national level

What Our Country Reports Cover

Each report delivers institutional-grade intelligence across: market sizing and forecasting, industry segmentation, competitive landscape, regulatory environment, macroeconomic context, and strategic recommendations — all grounded in conservative, multi-source validated methodology.

Institutional Rigour at the Most Competitive Pricing

Markets NXT offers country-specific research at the most competitive pricing in the industry, without compromising analytical standards. Our reports serve growth-stage businesses, investment firms, and consulting teams that need board-level intelligence without enterprise-level costs.
