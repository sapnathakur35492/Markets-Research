Privacy Policy

Markets NXT (“Markets NXT,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of personal and business information entrusted to us. As a provider of market intelligence, research, and consulting services, we recognize that responsible data handling is essential to our institutional credibility and client trust.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and disclose information when you visit our website, interact with our services, or communicate with us. This policy applies to website visitors, clients, subscribers, partners, and other stakeholders who engage with Markets NXT.

By accessing our website or using our services, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • Visitors to Markets NXT websites and digital platforms
  • Individuals submitting inquiries or requests
  • Clients and prospective clients
  • Report purchasers and subscribers
  • Event participants and webinar registrants
  • Business partners and collaborators

This policy covers personal and business contact information and related usage data but does not apply to third-party websites that may be linked from our platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in a limited and purpose-driven manner. Categories of information may include:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

You may voluntarily provide information when you:

  • Fill out contact forms
  • Request reports or proposals
  • Subscribe to newsletters or research updates
  • Register for briefings or events
  • Communicate with us by email or phone

This information may include: Name, Business email address, Organization name, Job title or function, Country or region, Phone number, Inquiry details and message content.

2.2 Business & Transaction Information

When you purchase reports or engage services, we may collect:

  • Billing contact details
  • Company information
  • Transaction records
  • Engagement correspondence
  • Service delivery preferences

Payment processing is typically handled through secure third-party payment providers. Markets NXT does not store full payment card details.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information for legitimate business and operational purposes, including:

3.1 Service Delivery

  • Responding to inquiries and requests
  • Delivering purchased reports and services
  • Managing consulting and research engagements
  • Providing client support

3.2 Communication

  • Sending requested research updates
  • Providing service-related communications
  • Sharing relevant insights or event invitations (where appropriate)

3.3 Operational Improvement

  • Improving website performance and usability
  • Enhancing research and service quality
  • Developing new offerings

3.4 Compliance & Protection

  • Meeting legal and regulatory requirements
  • Enforcing contractual rights
  • Protecting against misuse or unauthorized activity

We do not use personal data for unrelated purposes without appropriate notice or consent.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable under data protection laws, Markets NXT processes information under one or more of the following bases:

  • Legitimate business interest
  • Contractual necessity
  • User consent
  • Legal obligation
  • Protection of legitimate rights and interests

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Markets NXT may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance website functionality and understand usage behavior. Cookies may be used to:

  • Maintain session functionality
  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze traffic patterns
  • Improve site performance

Users may control cookie settings through their browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect certain website features.

6. Information Sharing & Disclosure

Markets NXT does not sell personal information to third parties. We may share information only under limited and controlled circumstances:

6.1 Service Providers

With trusted third-party vendors who support: Website hosting, Email delivery, Analytics services, Payment processing, CRM systems. These providers are contractually obligated to maintain confidentiality and security.

6.2 Legal & Regulatory Requirements

We may disclose information if required to: Comply with law or regulation, Respond to lawful requests, Protect legal rights, Prevent fraud or misuse.

6.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, restructuring, or asset transfer, information may be transferred subject to confidentiality protections.

7. Data Security Measures

Markets NXT maintains reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Security measures include:

  • Access controls and role restrictions
  • Secure data storage systems
  • Confidentiality agreements
  • Controlled document access
  • Internal data handling protocols

While no system can guarantee absolute security, we apply structured safeguards consistent with institutional standards.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal and business information only as long as necessary for: Service delivery, Contract fulfillment, Legal compliance, Legitimate business purposes.

Retention periods vary depending on the type of information and engagement context. Data no longer required is securely deleted or anonymized where practical.

9. International Data Transfers

Markets NXT operates through a global analyst and client network. Information may be processed in multiple jurisdictions. Where cross-border data transfer occurs, we apply appropriate safeguards and contractual protections.

10. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, individuals may have the right to:

  • Request access to their data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion where appropriate
  • Object to certain processing
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Requests may be submitted using the contact details below. We may verify identity before processing requests.

11. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. Markets NXT is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external sites. Users should review third-party privacy policies independently.

12. Children’s Privacy

Markets NXT services are intended for business and professional audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

13. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect operational, legal, or regulatory changes. Updated versions will be posted with a revised effective date. Continued use of our website after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
